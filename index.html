Web design also focuses on optimizing user experience (UX) and ensuring the site performs well across various devices and platforms.

Web designing is the process of planning, conceptualizing, and implementing the plan for designing a website in a way that is functional and offers a good user experience. User experience is central to the web designing process. Websites have an array of elements presented in ways that make them easy to navigate.

SEO

Search engine optimization (SEO) is the process of improving the quality and quantity of website traffic to a website or a web page from search engines.[1][2] SEO targets unpaid search traffic (usually referred to as "organic" results) rather than direct traffic, referral traffic, social media traffic, or paid traffic.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing a website or content to improve its visibility and ranking on search engine results pages (SERPs) for relevant queries. The higher a website ranks, the more organic traffic it is likely to receive. SEO is critical for driving traffic, increasing visibility, and achieving business goals online.
